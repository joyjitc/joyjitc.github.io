About Me

Hi, I am Joyjit Choudhury.

I am a Master of Science (Thesis) candidate in Computer Science at the University of Victoria (Sept 2024 – Dec 2025) with a GPA of 9.0/9. My research focuses on VLS Databases and distributed LLM agent orchestration.

I previously completed a Master of Applied Computer Science at Concordia University (Jan 2024 – Aug 2024) and a B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering from the National Institute of Technology Durgapur (2014 – 2018, GPA: 8.74/10).

My professional experience includes:

  • Graduate Teaching Assistant, University of Victoria — Jan 2025 to Apr 2025. Graded and supported instruction for CSC 429/529 Cryptography courses.
  • Senior Software Engineer (SDE‑3), Turvo (Lineage, Inc.) — Aug 2019 to Dec 2023. Integrated OpenRouteService and Nominatim APIs, modernized the internal map library, led critical feature development, and mentored new hires. Technologies: Java, Python, Elasticsearch, RabbitMQ, Redis.
  • Applications Engineer, Oracle — Jul 2018 to Aug 2019. Developed and maintained the Budget Control Module for Mercury, an in‑house supply chain planning tool. Technologies: Java, REST APIs, JAX‑RS, EJB, JPA, SQL, JavaScript.
  • Research Intern, Complex Networks Research Group (IIT Kharagpur) — Summer 2017. Created a C++ package for fitting parameters of sparse multidimensional Hawkes processes to support doctoral research.
